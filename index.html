This game of PONG is inspired by the duel on Mustafar from Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith between Anakin and Obi-Wan. I made the paddles appear to be lightsabers, one Jedi (blue) and one Sith (red). I want this game of pong to envoke a sense of intensity in the player as if they were dueling on Mustafar. You may change the difficulty of the game from Padawan, Jedi Knight, Jedi Master, and Chosen One by pressing '1' and cycling through them.
